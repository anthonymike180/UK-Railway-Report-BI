## 📊 Project Overview

This project delivers an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution analyzing UK railway operations to identify key performance indicators, optimize revenue streams, and improve passenger experience. Built with **Power BI**, **Power Query**, and **Excel**, this dashboard provides actionable insights for railway operators and stakeholders.

### 🎯 Business Objectives

- 📊 Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) for railway operations
- 📈 Analyze customer travel patterns and ticket purchasing behavior
- 🗺️ Visualize geographic distribution of railway usage across the UK
- ⏱️ Track delay rates and refund patterns to identify service issues
- 💰 Understand revenue streams through ticket type analysis

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎛️ Interactive KPI Cards
- **Average Customers Per Month**: 7.9K monthly active customers
- **Average Ticket Price**: $23.4 per ticket
- **Delayed Rate**: 13.2% of total trips
- **Refund Rate**: 3.5% of transactions
- **Standard vs First Class Pricing**: $20.7 (Standard) | $48.9 (First Class)

### 📈 Time Series Analysis
- Weekly trip volume tracking with trend lines
- Outlier detection for holiday periods (Good Friday, Easter Monday)
- Seasonal pattern identification

### 🗺️ Geographic Visualization
- Interactive map showing station concentrations across the UK
- Filterable by major stations:
  - Birmingham New Street
  - Manchester Piccadilly
  - Reading
  - York

### 🎫 Ticket Analytics
- **Ticket Class Distribution**: Standard (28.6K) vs First Class (3.06K)
- **Customer Segmentation**: Normal Day (22.67K) vs Weekend (8.99K) travelers
- **Railcard Usage**: None (20.92K), Adult (4.85K), Disabled (3.09K)

### 🛒 Purchase Behavior
- **Purchase Channels**: Online (18.52K) vs Station (13.13K)
- **Ticket Types**: Advance (17.56K), Off-Peak (8.75K), Anytime (5.34K)

# 🧠 Insights Discovered

### Passenger Behavior

-   Most tickets purchased are **Standard Class**.
-   **Advance tickets dominate sales**, suggesting passengers prefer
    cheaper early bookings.

### Purchase Channels

-   **Online purchases exceed station purchases**, indicating strong
    digital adoption.

### Service Reliability

-   The delay rate sits around **13%**, which could indicate operational
    inefficiencies.

### Refund Patterns

-   Refund requests remain relatively low at **3.5%**.

### Station Traffic

-   **Manchester Piccadilly and London Euston** are among the busiest
    departure stations.
